Technical Specification

HAPKE BRDF

Our shading model combines HAPKE BRDF1 and Single Scatter Albedo, providing scientifically accurate results. Texture each planet with albedo, roughness, height, and emission maps, all supported through a PBR workflow.

- Single Scatter Albedo2
- Textured Planets
- Use PBR workflow

- Albedo
- Roughness
- Height
- Emission

Planets

Planets with real-world measurements and scale them down for easier use. Multiple rotation and positioning options. Each sphere interacts with lighting, casting realistic shadows and indirect light effects on other planets.

- Input Real World Scale & Scale down
- Move and Rotate different methods
- Spheres, multiple planets cast shadows & indirect light

Phases

Each layer in your planet‚Äôs atmosphere can be fine-tuned for realistic light behavior, using the Draine Phase model with Cornette-Shanks, Rayleigh for molecular scattering, and Mie for aerosol scattering.

- For individual layers
- Draine Phase model3

- Includes Cornette-Shanks4
- Molecular Phase (Rayleigh)
- Aerosol Phase (Mie)

6 Layer atmosphere

Rich atmospheres with six interactive layers that scatter, absorb, and emit light naturally. Each layer casts shadows, with texture-driven density and height profiles for added realism, including options for stunning procedural auroras.

- Layers Sum and Absorb
- Single Scattering Model
- Scatter, Absorb & Emit
- Cast shadows
- Texture-driven Density & Height Profile
- Procedural Aurora texture

Spectral rendering

Precise light wavelengths to achieve accurate color representation in your scenes.

- Input coefficients for 470, 650, 700 ùëõm wavelengths

Rings

Stunning, customizable rings that cast realistic shadows onto the planet and atmosphere. This creates a rich interplay of light and shadow, perfect for cinematic planetary visuals.

- Shadows
- Indirect Light

Background Stars

A procedurally generated star background to offer an out of the box a universe-like look.
